Before you send

What helps the most

  • Tool name — tell us which calculator or category you were using.
  • What happened — what you expected vs what you saw.
  • Your inputs — include the numbers you entered when relevant.
  • Screenshot if needed — especially helpful for layout or display issues.

The more specific the message, the easier it is to troubleshoot quickly.

Response

What to expect

ScopedLabs is intentionally lean. Messages are reviewed as availability allows, with priority going to support issues and tool problems first.

No live chat. No ticket portal. Just a direct line for real questions.
