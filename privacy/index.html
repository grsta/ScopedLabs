Privacy Policy

How ScopedLabs handles account, payment, support, and saved-result data.

Last updated: May 2, 2026

ScopedLabs provides engineering planning tools, calculators, analyzers, saved snapshots, and export/report features through scopedlabs.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate ScopedLabs and provide access to free and paid tool features. This may include:

  • Email address and authentication information used for passwordless sign-in.
  • Purchase, entitlement, and checkout status information needed to unlock paid categories.
  • Support or contact messages you send to ScopedLabs.
  • Saved snapshots, report metadata, and tool results you choose to save to your account.
  • Basic technical information such as browser, device, IP address, log data, and security events.
  • Local browser data, such as session storage or local storage, used to support tool flow, account state, and saved-result behavior.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. ScopedLabs does not store full credit card numbers or full payment credentials on its own servers. Stripe may collect and process payment, billing, fraud-prevention, and transaction information according to its own legal and privacy terms.

How we use information

We use information to operate the site, authenticate accounts, provide category unlocks, process purchases, save snapshots when requested, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, improve reliability, and maintain site security.

Service providers

ScopedLabs may use trusted service providers to operate the site and related services, including payment processing, authentication, hosting, database storage, analytics, security, email delivery, and infrastructure operations. These providers may process information in the United States or other locations where they operate.

Cookies and browser storage

ScopedLabs may use cookies, local storage, and session storage to keep you signed in, remember tool flow context, support checkout and category unlock behavior, save local snapshots, and maintain site security. You can control cookies and storage through your browser settings, but some account or checkout features may not work correctly if storage is disabled.

Saved snapshots and tool results

If you save snapshots or reports, the saved information may include the tool name, input values, calculated outputs, report title, project/client labels you enter, notes, and timestamps. Do not save confidential project information unless you are comfortable storing it in your ScopedLabs account.

Sharing information

ScopedLabs does not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers as needed to operate the site, comply with law, prevent fraud or abuse, resolve disputes, protect rights and security, or complete a transaction you request.

Data retention

We keep account, purchase, support, and saved-result information for as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve security. You may request deletion of account-related information by contacting ScopedLabs, subject to legal, security, and transaction-record requirements.

Your choices

You may choose not to create an account, not to purchase paid access, and not to save snapshots. You may also clear local browser storage, unsubscribe from non-essential messages if offered, or contact ScopedLabs about access, correction, or deletion requests.

Children

ScopedLabs is intended for professional and educational use by adults and is not directed to children under 13.

Security

ScopedLabs uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no website, payment system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

Changes to this policy

ScopedLabs may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@scopedlabs.com.
