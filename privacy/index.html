Privacy Policy

How ScopedLabs handles account, payment, support, saved snapshot, and browser storage information.

Last updated

May 2, 2026

ScopedLabs provides engineering planning tools, calculators, analyzers, saved snapshots, and export/report features through scopedlabs.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information

Information we collect

ScopedLabs collects only the information needed to operate the site, provide account access, process purchases, support saved reports, and respond to user requests.

  • Email address and passwordless sign-in information.
  • Purchase, checkout, and entitlement status needed to unlock paid categories.
  • Support or contact messages you send to ScopedLabs.
  • Saved snapshots, report metadata, custom notes, and tool results you choose to save.
  • Basic technical information such as browser, device, IP address, log data, and security events.
  • Local browser data used for account state, checkout flow, tool flow context, and saved-result behavior.
Payments

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. ScopedLabs does not store full credit card numbers or full payment credentials on its own servers. Stripe may collect and process payment, billing, fraud-prevention, and transaction information according to its own legal and privacy terms.

Use

How we use information

ScopedLabs uses information to operate the site, authenticate accounts, provide category unlocks, process purchases, save snapshots when requested, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, improve reliability, and maintain site security.

Providers

Service providers

ScopedLabs may use trusted service providers for payment processing, authentication, hosting, database storage, analytics, security, email delivery, and infrastructure operations. These providers may process information in the United States or other locations where they operate.

Browser storage

Cookies and local storage

ScopedLabs may use cookies, local storage, and session storage to keep you signed in, remember tool flow context, support checkout and category unlock behavior, save local snapshots, and maintain site security. Some account or checkout features may not work correctly if browser storage is disabled.

Snapshots

Saved snapshots and tool results

If you save snapshots or reports, the saved information may include the tool name, input values, calculated outputs, report title, project or client labels you enter, notes, and timestamps. Do not save confidential project information unless you are comfortable storing it in your ScopedLabs account.

Sharing

How information may be shared

ScopedLabs does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers as needed to operate the site, comply with law, prevent fraud or abuse, resolve disputes, protect rights and security, or complete a transaction you request.

Retention

Data retention and choices

ScopedLabs keeps account, purchase, support, and saved-result information for as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve security.

You may choose not to create an account, not to purchase paid access, and not to save snapshots. You may contact ScopedLabs about access, correction, or deletion requests, subject to legal, security, and transaction-record requirements.

Security

Security and updates

ScopedLabs uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no website, payment system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

ScopedLabs may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date.
