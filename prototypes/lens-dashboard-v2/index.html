Focal Demand

Pressure is in the Risk band, indicating the selected focal length is too long for the target size at this distance. Reduce focal length or increase sensor width to move into the Watch or Healthy range.

Why Risk?
  • The design is asking one camera to hold a narrow field of view from a long distance.
  • The required focal length is above the preferred planning band.
Likely Drivers
  • Long camera-to-target distance.
  • Narrow target width.
  • Sensor width limits horizontal field of view.
Path to Healthy
  • Reduce focal length to ≤ 18 mm target range.
  • Verify sensor width or consider a larger sensor.
  • Recalculate pressure and confirm in Watch/Healthy band.
Follow-up Checks
  • Validate with field test or high-fidelity simulation.
  • Confirm target visibility in the live view.
  • Document final lens choice.
⚙Engineering Interpretation

The system is over-magnifying relative to the required detail. This increases sensitivity to vibration, atmospheric degradation, and mounting error while reducing field of view.
