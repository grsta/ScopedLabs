Scenario Recommendation

Split coverage gives the strongest optical improvement and moves the result cleanly into the preferred planning band. If camera count or infrastructure cost is constrained, closer camera placement is the next best planning lever to evaluate. Before choosing either path, validate the actual field of view, required detail objective, mounting options, and manufacturer lens availability.
