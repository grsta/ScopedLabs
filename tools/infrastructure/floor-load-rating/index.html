Floor Load Rating

Part of a Design Flow

This tool continues the Infrastructure design flow

Use this step to check whether the rack footprint still preserves real structural margin after equipment load and layout decisions have already been established.

This is where total rack weight turns into actual pressure on the floor, and where concentrated loading can become the real deployment limiter even when the room still looks spacious.

Best for: verifying structural safety and identifying when point loading approaches floor-rating limits before deployment.
