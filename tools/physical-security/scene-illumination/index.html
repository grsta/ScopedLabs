Planning Inputs

Estimate lighting demand across the protected area so the rest of the camera design flow starts from a realistic scene-illumination baseline.

Choose a lighting goal to see the recommended target range and lumen impact.
Lighting depth is separate from camera distance.
Use the depth of the area being evaluated for usable light. A remote pole, offset fixture, or dark camera location may not light the camera target zone even if the camera target is far away.
Choose fixture/layout and maintenance assumptions to see the effective planning factor.

Scene Illumination Assistant

Review the lighting baseline, planning factor pressure, and recommended correction path before carrying the scene into mounting-height decisions.

Enter values and press Estimate Lighting.
