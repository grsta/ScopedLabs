Battery Sizing

Best for: sizing a simple DC battery requirement when you know your load and runtime target and want to account for conversion losses.

Results

Enter values and press Calculate.

Model: if watts are entered, current is derived from voltage. If amps are entered, watts are derived from voltage. Efficiency accounts for inverter, converter, or discharge losses before final battery capacity is calculated.

Notes / Assumptions
  • If you enter Watts, amps are derived as: Amps = Watts ÷ Volts
  • If you enter Amps, watts are derived as: Watts = Volts × Amps
  • Efficiency accounts for inverter / driver losses and battery discharge inefficiency.
