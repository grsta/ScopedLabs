Ambient Rise Estimator

Part of a Design Flow

This tool continues the Thermal design flow

Use this step to estimate how much the local ambient temperature rises once the available airflow is asked to absorb the current thermal load.

This is where airflow quantity starts translating into a real environmental temperature consequence before you move on to exhaust conditions.

Best for: predicting how much the environment temperature will rise given airflow and heat load before validating exhaust conditions.
