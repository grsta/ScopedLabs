Room Cooling Capacity

Part of a Design Flow

This tool completes the Thermal design flow

Use this final step to convert total system heat into the room-level cooling capacity required to keep the environment stable with planning margin.

This is where the full thermal chain is validated at the infrastructure layer so you can compare the modeled load against real HVAC support instead of relying only on rack-level assumptions.

Best for: validating whether the room cooling system can handle total thermal load and determining required HVAC capacity.
