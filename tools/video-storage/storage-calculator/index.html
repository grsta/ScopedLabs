Storage Planner

Estimate realistic video storage needs using camera count, bitrate, recording behavior, retention period, and scene activity.

This planner estimates per-camera daily storage, total daily load, and retention storage. Unlike simple storage calculators, it also compares continuous recording to motion and event-based recording so you can plan closer to real-world use.
