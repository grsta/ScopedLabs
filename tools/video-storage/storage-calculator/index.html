Storage Calculator

Part of a Design Flow

This tool continues the Video & Storage design flow

Use this step to convert the imported bitrate assumption into daily and retained storage demand. This is where the stream estimate becomes a real capacity target.

It gives the rest of the pipeline a concrete storage burden so retention, RAID protection, and survivability are all evaluated against the same baseline load.

Best for: quick NVR sizing and rough storage estimates.
