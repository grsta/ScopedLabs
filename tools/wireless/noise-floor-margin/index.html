Noise Floor Margin

Part of a Design Flow

This tool continues the Wireless design flow

Use this step to verify whether the RF environment still has enough signal-to-noise margin to support stable client service after the channel plan is considered.

It helps show whether the design is now being limited by noise, weak signal, or simply by how tightly the current SNR tracks the required target.

Best for: validating whether the RF environment has enough margin to support stable client service before you size client density and AP loading.

What this models
